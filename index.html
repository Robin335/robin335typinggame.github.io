  • How to Improve Typing Speed

    • Game Rules

    1. Objective: Type quickly and accurately to meet level requirements.

    2. Choose Level: Select a difficulty; each level has unique speed and accuracy targets.

    3. Start: Enter your name and click "Start Game." The timer begins immediately.

    4. Progress: Your WPM, accuracy, and mistakes are tracked live.

    5. Level Up: Meet WPM and accuracy goals to advance to tougher levels.

    6. Game Over: The game ends when time runs out; when you comlate a level your results will pop up on the right side of the game.

    7. Mute: Click "Mute" to turn off sounds.
